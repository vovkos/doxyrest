PCM timestamp mode

Enum Values

SND_PCM_TSTAMP_NONE

No timestamp

SND_PCM_TSTAMP_ENABLE

Update timestamp at every hardware position update

SND_PCM_TSTAMP_MMAP

Equivalent with SND_PCM_TSTAMP_ENABLE, just for compatibility with older versions
